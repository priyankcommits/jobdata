Experienced Front End Engineer
Loveseat Inc. - San Diego, CA
$75,000 a year

Experienced Front-End Developer Position:
We're looking for a Front-End Developer who wants to be part of a startup from the ground floor and have a meaningful impact on its growth. You should be an HTML / CSS / Javascript ninja and be up-to-date with the latest methodologies.
We are a funded startup focusing on vintage furniture with a technical twist based here in San Diego. You would be joining as the third engineer on our tech team and work directly with the founders. We are located off Clairemont Mesa Blvd by the 805. See our website at www.loveseat.com for more about what we do.
At Loveseat, you will have a crucial role in work on our website, as well as our Phonegap / Cordova-based mobile products. You will be involved in design planning and will be expected to reproduce those designs in code.
This full-time in-office position will go to a motivated self-starter. We want someone on our team who works hard, but can also have fun doing it. Please bring big smiles and a sense of humor. This shouldn't feel like a job.

Some things we are looking for:
Experience shipping real products that people use.
A desire to collaborate, share ideas, ask for help, and offer help.
Interest in learning new technologies and teaching others.
A desire to work hard and win.
A willingness to roll up your sleeves and GSD.
Experience with Django template language is a big plus, but not required.

Other stuff to know:
We pay competitively.
We focus on results over hours spent working.
We have a generous stock option plan for tech team members.
